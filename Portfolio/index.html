Introduction - Aashutosh Dahal

Hi, My name is Aashutosh Dahal and I am from a South Asian country named Nepal. I am currently an Information Management Student who is trying to explore all the areas and fields I get my hand into. I do traditional as well as digital art and currently learning graphics design along with web design. I am a guy in his early 20’s trying to find where I am gonna end up in life after 5 years. The journey so far has been more highs and less down, but let’s see what the future holds for me.

My Field of Interest

Traditional Art

I have been passionate about drawing since a young age, I used to draw many simple drawings from newspapers and picture books. In 2018 I started doing it properly, I took classes, learned from different sites, got all the materials required, and gave my everything for this.

Digital Art

I got introduced to digital drawing in late 2021 when I got access to a digital tablet and I have never looked back since then. I am thoroughly enjoying the drawing process on the digital platform and will continue to do so.

Web Design

After I completed my 12th, I decided to learn web design and that was probably one of the best decision . I took courses from Coursera and took help from different websites. I even worked as a freelancer for a few months and completed many projects.

Graphics Design

I was interested in graphics design for a long time. I learned Photoshop and illustrator when I was in school. I took it as a hobby and never got too deep into it but recently I started taking a graphics design course and am planning to make a career out of it.
